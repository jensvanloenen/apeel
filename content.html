About Us

Mission Statement

The first step in developing our brand is to develop a vision for it. What message does our brand want to send out to the world? Because we have developed a habit of always wanting more in the twenty-first century, contentment became our focal point. We created the following mission statement with this in mind: “In a world where people feel the need to continuously search for something new to stay satisfied, we believe in appreciating that which you already have, because only then can we live a content life.”

Values

Our main value, as reflected in the mission statement, is contentment. In addition to this, A'peel is also both defined by and stives for:

    - minimalism
    - simplicity
    - timelessness
    - appreciativeness

Brand Name

Our brand name, A'peel, is derived from both our mission statement and the products our brand offers. Peeling back your unnecessary wants and appreciating what you already have. The name symbolizes the beauty that already exists around you. To appreciate, to be content. Our items are also manufactured from recycled fruit peels. Creating items that are simple and timeless, and that appeal to our intended target demographic.

Brand Identity

A’peel has a simple name that is memorable and unique. The logo provides instant brand recognition as the customers can have several associations with the brand, for example the layers in the logo, refer not only to the brand’s mission statement but also the fabrication of the brands products. Consequently, A’peel has the potential to create a strong brand identity.

Persona

In order to connect with our ideal target audience, we need to create a personality that our ideal target audience can relate to. A’peel-as-a-person would be a minimalist, who appreciates the small things in life. A’peel is very honest, innovative, caring, and frugal. A’peel does not follow any seasonal trends, she has a timeless and simplistic style.
